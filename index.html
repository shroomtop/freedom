HOW THEY CONTROL YOU — THE CYCLE

  1. Algorithmic Tracking: Every scroll, like, pause, and click is tracked to profile your mood, triggers, and habits.
  2. Personalized Manipulation: Feeds show what triggers the strongest emotion—even if it’s outrage, envy, or anxiety—to keep you scrolling.
  3. Endless Dopamine Loops: Infinite feeds, reels, and stories trigger reward cycles that hack your attention span.
  4. Social Validation Traps: Notifications, likes, and comments are engineered to reinforce checking and comparing, fueling FOMO and anxiety.
  5. Psychological Conditioning: Over time, you internalize platform values—more distraction, less critical thinking, less sovereignty.

HOW TO BREAK FREE — 100% LEGAL & EASY

  • Delete or Limit the Apps: Remove or log out of Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok from your phone. Use only the browser if necessary (no autoplay, no notifications).
  • Block Algorithmic Feeds: News Feed Eradicator (Chrome/Edge/Firefox): Hides main feeds—removes the algorithmic loop.
    Freedom.to: Block distracting apps/sites across devices (iOS, Android, PC, Mac).
  • Silence All Notifications: On iOS/Android: Go to Settings → Notifications → Turn OFF for all social media. Zero buzz, zero interruption.
  • Set Strict Time Limits: Use built-in Digital Wellbeing (Android) or Screen Time (iOS) to limit use per day. Enforce hard stops.
  • Switch to Direct Feeds: Use Feedly or your favorite newsletters/RSS—only content YOU choose, not the algorithm.
  • Weekly Audit: Every week, ask: “What apps/sites actually help me think, build, or connect?” Uninstall, unfollow, or mute anything that doesn’t add real value.
  • Try a Detox: Go 3–7 days with zero algorithmic social media. Journal the difference in your mood, focus, and ideas.
